Employee Data Analysis using Hive (Hadoop)

Objective: Analyzed employee data to gain insights into performance, salary distribution, and department-wise headcount.

Tools Used: Hive, Hadoop

  • Retrieved key employee details including ID, gender, marital status, education level, salary, bonus, and performance rating.
  • Filtered data to identify top-performing employees with a rating of 4 or higher.
  • Sorted employees by salary to determine the top 5 highest-paid employees.
  • Computed salary statistics including minimum, maximum, and average salaries.
  • Classified employees into "Low," "Medium," and "High" salary groups.
  • Grouped employees by department to analyze workforce distribution.

Outcome: Provided workforce insights through Hive queries, improving decision-making related to employee performance and salary trends.
